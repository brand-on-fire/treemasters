ABOUT US

Tad Jacobs founded TREEMASTERS with three very simple principles; Honesty, Integrity and Craftsmanship and after thirty years in business those principles continue to be our pledge to our clients.

At TREEMASTERS we show pride in providing the highest quality tree care for our valued clients. Every day we aim to maintain our commitments to serving our clients and preserving the environment. High quality customer service comes first and foremost from our dedicated staff. We provide the superior quality tree care experience TREEMASTERS has been best known for.

SERVICES

Since our beginning in 1987, TREEMASTERS has had the pleasure of servicing over 30,000 clients around the bay area.

TREEMASTERS offers a comprehensive variety of high quality tree care services. Our goal is to serve our clients with Honesty, Integrity, and Craftsmanship while maintaining our commitment to protecting the environment. Our team of dedicated professionals will complete your job quickly and efficiently, and leave your property clean and free of debris.

TREEMASTERS was the first GREEN CERTIFIED tree service in Marin County. Because of our commitment to the environment, TREEMASTERS uses a biodegradable soy based lubricant on our chainsaws.
